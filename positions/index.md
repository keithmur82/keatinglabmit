## Join the Keating Lab

We are always looking for talented and curious people at all levels.

—

### Graduate Students
We accept students through the MIT
[Biology](https://biology.mit.edu/graduate/) and
[Biological Engineering](https://be.mit.edu/academic-programs/graduate-programs)
PhD programs. Rotation students are welcome — email Amy to set one up.

—

### Postdoctoral Researchers
We have openings for postdoctoral researchers with backgrounds in:
- Computational protein design
- Structural biology  
- Machine learning applied to biology

Please send a CV, brief statement of interest, and contact information
for three references to **keating@mit.edu**

—

### Undergraduate Students
MIT undergrads interested in UROP positions should email Amy directly
with a brief description of your background and interests.

—

*The Keating Lab is committed to building a diverse and
inclusive research environment.*
